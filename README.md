Total Internal Reflection | الانعكاس الكلي الداخلي
English Description
An interactive educational application for 9th-grade physics covering Total Internal Reflection and the Critical Angle phenomenon. The app features:

Interactive 3D Simulation: Control the angle of incidence of a laser beam and observe refraction, critical angle, and total internal reflection in real-time using Three.js
Critical Angle Calculator: Compute the critical angle for various transparent media (water, glass, diamond, etc.) using Snell's Law
Virtual Experiment: Step-by-step simulation of the semicircular glass block experiment with tools, procedures, observations, and conclusions
Interactive Examples: Determine whether TIR conditions are met, calculate critical angles, and complete ray path diagrams
Optical Phenomena: Animated explanations of mirages (inferior & superior), rainbow formation, optical fibers, and diamond brilliance
Assessment Quiz: 8 multiple-choice questions with instant feedback, explanations, and scoring
Key Formulas:

Snell's Law: n₁ sin θ₁ = n₂ sin θ₂
Critical Angle: sin θc = n₂ / n₁ (or sin θc = 1/n for air interface)
Technologies: Streamlit, Three.js, HTML5 Canvas, CSS3
